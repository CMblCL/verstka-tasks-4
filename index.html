Snap Prices On Standard Pianos

Many Bargains in Our Closing Out Sale of New Instruments

Every day is a big day in this sale – we are putting out more pianos than ever before, breaking all previous records. The success of this sale is the result of honest advertising, and fair pricing of high grade instruments.

We have to close out six agencies — every new piano outside the Steinway, Xranich & Bach, Sohmer and Kurtzmann must be moved off our floors at once. In order to accomplish our purpose all Emerson, Estey, Sterling, Hantington, Laffargue, Mendelssohn pianos must go at cost or less than cost.

We have closed all our branch stores in Southern California – this means that over forty pianos have been sent to the store in Los Angeles to be disposed of. These instruments so into our closing out sale at extremely low prices. Here are some of the big specials.

$475 Emerson Pianos at $230.
$475 Sterling Pianos at $290.
$450 Sterling Pianos at $250.
$350 Huntington Pianos at $195. $325 Mendelssohn Pianos at $185.

Remember, all the above are absolutely new instruments. Our decision to close them out gives won the greatest plano opportunity ever presented in this city. Any instrument in the sale can be bought on easy terms. No matter how little you can afford to pay each month we can arrange satisfactory terms
